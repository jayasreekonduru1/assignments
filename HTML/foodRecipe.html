Sweet Potato Pie Recipe

Ingredients of Sweet Potato Pie

  • 450 Gram Yams (red skinned)
  • 150 gram Condensed Milk
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • 1 small Egg
  • A pinch of Cinnamon
  • To decorate Marshmallows
How to make Sweet Potato Pie:
1. Bake the yams until they have softened completely - they should be extremely tender. Cool, discard the skin and then blend the flesh into a paste.
2. Mix the paste with the remaining ingredients and whisk well until everything is smooth and perfectly blended - you don't want any lumpy bits in your filling.
3. Add the filling into your crust and bake(about 50 minutes to 1 hour). Make sure to let it cool, then place in the fridge overnight.
4. You are ready to serve the Sweet Potato Pie.
